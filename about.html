About us

LMS FOR LEARNING is a modern Learning Management System created to help students learn faster, smarter, and more efficiently through online courses, videos, quizzes, certificates, and progress tracking.

Creator

This application was created by FESTO ERICK MAPUNDA, a Computer and Communication Engineering student at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, Tamil Nadu, India.

The purpose of developing this LMS is to improve the learning experience for students by providing an easy-to-use platform where they can access structured courses, interactive resources, educational videos, and self-assessment tools — all in one place.

Festo designed this LMS with the goal of making digital learning more accessible, organized, and enjoyable for all learners.
